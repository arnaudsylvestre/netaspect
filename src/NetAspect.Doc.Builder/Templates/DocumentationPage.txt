               <h1 id="kinds">Interceptors</h1>

            <p>An interceptor is a method declared in the aspect and which will be called at certain moment after the weaving. In NetAspect, you can have 3 kinds of interceptors :</p>
			<ul>
               <li><b>Method interceptor</b> : the interceptor will be executed in the method (before the code of the method for example)</li>
               <li><b>Instruction interceptor</b> : the interceptor will be executed before or after an instruction (before calling a method for example)</li>
               <li><b>Parameter interceptor</b> : the interceptor will be executed in the method but the aspect is placed on a parameter</li>
            </ul>


            <p>Some parameters can be added to the interceptor to get more information</p>
